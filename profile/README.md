
# ReadShade

## About Us

### Who?

ReadShade is brought to you by a dedicated team of individuals: [BlueSkyDefender](https://github.com/BlueSkyDefender), [papadanku](https://github.com/papadanku), [PHARTGAMES](https://github.com/PHARTGAMES).

### What?

ReadShade is a collaborative effort to create a comprehensive guide website for ReShade, a post-processing injector for games and video software. Our aim is to provide clear, accessible, and in-depth information to help users get the most out of ReShade.

### When?

The ReadShade project officially began in October 2024.

### Where?

ReadShade originated from a community of add-on and shader developers within the broader ReShade ecosystem.

### Why?

Our motivation behind ReadShade is to offer a neutral, lightweight, and preservable guide that educates users. We strive to present information objectively, ensuring it remains easily accessible and relevant over time.

### How?

We welcome contributions from the community! If you're interested in getting involved, please refer to our contributing guidelines (if available) or reach out to the team members.

## Project Status

ReadShade is currently under active development. We are continually working to expand and refine our content.

## Technologies Used

The ReadShade website is built using the following technologies:

- **Sphinx**: For generating beautiful and functional documentation from reStructuredText sources.
- **Python**: The primary programming language used for scripting and Sphinx extensions.
- **reStructuredText**: The markup language used for writing the content of the guide.
- **Google Gemini CLI**: Utilized for documentation generation and revision.
